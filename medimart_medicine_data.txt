MEDIMART MEDICINE DATABASE - COMPREHENSIVE MEDICINE INFORMATION

==========================================
MEDICINE: PARACETAMOL (Acetaminophen)
==========================================
Generic Name: Paracetamol
Brand Names: Crocin, Dolo, Calpol
Category: Analgesic, Antipyretic

USES:
- Relief from mild to moderate pain (headache, toothache, body ache)
- Reduces fever
- Safe for general use in adults and children

DOSAGE GUIDELINES:
- Adults: 500-1000mg every 4-6 hours (max 4000mg/day)
- Children: As per weight and age (consult doctor)

SIDE EFFECTS:
- Generally well-tolerated
- Rare: Nausea, skin rash, liver damage (overdose)

PREGNANCY WARNING: SAFE - Generally considered safe during pregnancy when used as directed. Always consult doctor before use.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Severe liver disease
- Allergy to paracetamol

HINDI INFO:
नाम: पैरासिटामोल
उपयोग: दर्द और बुखार की दवा
सावधानी: गर्भावस्था में सुरक्षित लेकिन डॉक्टर से सलाह लें

==========================================
MEDICINE: IBUPROFEN
==========================================
Generic Name: Ibuprofen
Brand Names: Brufen, Advil, Combiflam
Category: NSAID (Non-Steroidal Anti-Inflammatory Drug)

USES:
- Pain relief (headache, dental pain, menstrual cramps)
- Reduces inflammation
- Fever reduction
- Arthritis pain management

DOSAGE GUIDELINES:
- Adults: 200-400mg every 4-6 hours (max 1200mg/day without prescription)
- Take with food to avoid stomach upset

SIDE EFFECTS:
- Stomach upset, nausea
- Heartburn, dizziness
- Rare: Stomach ulcers, kidney problems

PREGNANCY WARNING: AVOID - Not recommended during pregnancy, especially in third trimester. May cause complications. Consult doctor immediately.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Active stomach ulcers
- Severe heart disease
- Third trimester of pregnancy
- Aspirin allergy

HINDI INFO:
नाम: आइबुप्रोफेन
उपयोग: दर्द और सूजन की दवा
चेतावनी: गर्भावस्था में उपयोग न करें, खासकर आखिरी तिमाही में

==========================================
MEDICINE: ASPIRIN (Acetylsalicylic Acid)
==========================================
Generic Name: Aspirin
Brand Names: Disprin, Ecosprin
Category: NSAID, Antiplatelet

USES:
- Pain and fever relief
- Reduces inflammation
- Blood thinner (prevents heart attacks and strokes)
- Reduces risk of blood clots

DOSAGE GUIDELINES:
- Pain relief: 300-600mg every 4-6 hours
- Heart protection: 75-150mg daily (low dose)
- Take with food

SIDE EFFECTS:
- Stomach irritation
- Increased bleeding risk
- Allergic reactions (rash, breathing problems)

PREGNANCY WARNING: AVOID - Not safe during pregnancy, especially third trimester. May cause bleeding complications and harm to baby. Avoid unless specifically prescribed.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Children under 16 (risk of Reye's syndrome)
- Active bleeding disorders
- Stomach ulcers
- Pregnancy (third trimester)

HINDI INFO:
नाम: एस्पिरिन
उपयोग: दर्द, बुखार और रक्त को पतला करने की दवा
चेतावनी: गर्भावस्था में बिल्कुल न लें, बच्चों को न दें

==========================================
MEDICINE: METFORMIN
==========================================
Generic Name: Metformin Hydrochloride
Brand Names: Glycomet, Glucophage
Category: Anti-diabetic (Biguanide)

USES:
- Type 2 Diabetes management
- Controls blood sugar levels
- May help with weight management in diabetic patients
- PCOS (Polycystic Ovary Syndrome) treatment

DOSAGE GUIDELINES:
- Starting dose: 500mg once or twice daily with meals
- Maintenance: 500-2000mg per day in divided doses
- Must be taken with food

SIDE EFFECTS:
- Nausea, diarrhea (common initially)
- Stomach upset
- Metallic taste
- Rare: Lactic acidosis (serious)

PREGNANCY WARNING: CONSULT DOCTOR - May be used during pregnancy under medical supervision for gestational diabetes. Self-medication not recommended.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Severe kidney disease
- Liver disease
- Heart failure
- Heavy alcohol use

HINDI INFO:
नाम: मेटफॉर्मिन
उपयोग: मधुमेह (शुगर) की दवा
सावधानी: गर्भावस्था में केवल डॉक्टर की सलाह से लें

==========================================
MEDICINE: AMOXICILLIN
==========================================
Generic Name: Amoxicillin
Brand Names: Mox, Amoxil, Novamox
Category: Antibiotic (Penicillin group)

USES:
- Bacterial infections (respiratory, ear, throat, urinary)
- Dental infections
- Skin infections
- H. pylori treatment (with other medicines)

DOSAGE GUIDELINES:
- Adults: 250-500mg every 8 hours OR 500-875mg every 12 hours
- Complete full course even if feeling better
- Take with or without food

SIDE EFFECTS:
- Diarrhea, nausea
- Skin rash
- Yeast infections
- Allergic reactions (serious if penicillin allergy)

PREGNANCY WARNING: GENERALLY SAFE - Considered safe during pregnancy when prescribed by doctor. Commonly used antibiotic in pregnancy.

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Penicillin allergy
- History of liver problems with amoxicillin
- Infectious mononucleosis

HINDI INFO:
नाम: अमोक्सिसिलिन
उपयोग: बैक्टीरिया संक्रमण की दवा (एंटीबायोटिक)
सावधानी: गर्भावस्था में आमतौर पर सुरक्षित, डॉक्टर की सलाह से लें

==========================================
GENERAL HEALTH TIPS / सामान्य स्वास्थ्य सुझाव
==========================================

ENGLISH:
1. Always complete the full course of antibiotics even if you feel better
2. Never share prescription medicines with others
3. Store medicines in cool, dry place away from children
4. Check expiry dates before consuming any medicine
5. Pregnant women should consult doctor before taking any medicine
6. Take medicines with food if they cause stomach upset
7. Inform your doctor about all medicines you are currently taking
8. Never exceed recommended dosage without medical advice

HINDI:
1. एंटीबायोटिक्स का पूरा कोर्स करें, भले ही आप बेहतर महसूस करें
2. कभी भी दूसरों के साथ अपनी दवाएं साझा न करें
3. दवाओं को ठंडी, सूखी जगह पर बच्चों से दूर रखें
4. कोई भी दवा लेने से पहले समाप्ति तिथि जांचें
5. गर्भवती महिलाओं को कोई भी दवा लेने से पहले डॉक्टर से परामर्श लेना चाहिए
6. यदि दवाएं पेट खराब करती हैं तो भोजन के साथ लें
7. अपने डॉक्टर को उन सभी दवाओं के बारे में बताएं जो आप वर्तमान में ले रहे हैं
8. चिकित्सा सलाह के बिना अनुशंसित खुराक से अधिक न लें

==========================================
PREGNANCY SAFETY SUMMARY
==========================================

SAFE IN PREGNANCY:
- Paracetamol (with doctor consultation)
- Amoxicillin (when prescribed)

AVOID IN PREGNANCY:
- Ibuprofen (especially third trimester)
- Aspirin (especially third trimester)

CONSULT DOCTOR:
- Metformin (may be used under supervision)
- All other medicines not listed above

IMPORTANT: Every pregnancy is different. Always inform your healthcare provider that you are pregnant before taking any medicine.

गर्भावस्था में सुरक्षा सारांश:
गर्भावस्था में सुरक्षित: पैरासिटामोल (डॉक्टर से पूछकर), अमोक्सिसिलिन
गर्भावस्था में बचें: आइबुप्रोफेन, एस्पिरिन
डॉक्टर से परामर्श लें: मेटफॉर्मिन और अन्य सभी दवाएं
